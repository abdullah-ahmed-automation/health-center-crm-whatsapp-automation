# Health Center CRM & WhatsApp Follow-up Automation

A practical workflow automation case study for organizing leads, client follow-up, appointments, WhatsApp communication, reviews, renewals, and retention for a health and nutrition center.

## Project Overview

This project was designed for a health and nutrition center that needed a clearer system to manage leads, appointments, body scan visits, client communication, subscriptions, renewals, and follow-up.

The goal was to reduce scattered manual follow-up and create a more organized workflow using Google Sheets CRM, WhatsApp follow-up logic, appointment reminders, review requests, and client reactivation flows.

## Problem

The center was managing many operational tasks manually, including lead tracking, appointment reminders, scan result follow-up, missed visits, reviews, renewals, and client communication.

This created several issues:

- Leads from multiple platforms were hard to track
- Appointment follow-up depended heavily on manual reminders
- Missed visits were not always followed up quickly
- Client communication was scattered across different channels
- Renewals and inactive clients needed a clearer follow-up process
- The team needed a simple CRM view for daily operations

## Solution

I designed a Google Sheets CRM and WhatsApp follow-up workflow structure to organize client data, lead sources, appointment status, visit follow-up, missed-visit messages, review requests, subscription tracking, renewal follow-up, and reactivation campaigns.

The system was designed to be practical, easy for the team to use, and focused on improving daily follow-up and client retention.

## Main Workflows

- Lead tracking from TikTok, Instagram, Snapchat, Facebook, and WhatsApp
- Google Sheets CRM for leads, bookings, attendance, subscriptions, renewals, and package progress
- Appointment reminders before body scan visits
- WhatsApp delivery of body scan result follow-up messages
- Missed-visit follow-up with rescheduling options
- Google review request messages after visits
- Client reactivation workflows for inactive clients and expired subscriptions
- Renewal follow-up and subscription tracking
- Progress-based WhatsApp messages for fat loss, muscle gain, weak results, and motivation
- Weekly educational WhatsApp messages
- AI-assisted social media content planning for posts, captions, reels, ad copy, content calendars, and reports
